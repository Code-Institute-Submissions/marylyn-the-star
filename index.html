Duncan Hopper
Writer

Duncan the author of Marilyn The Star has had a distinguished career in business. His first love however is writing plays and books. His play Lyn Roe was a hit at the Edinburgh Festival, I and Lady Montagu the second and Helen's Shadow his third, also did well. He has specially adapted this for the Brighton Festival.
